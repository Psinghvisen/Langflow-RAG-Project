
=== Chat Logs ===
User: Hi, I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
Support: No worries! You can reset your password by clicking on “Forgot Password” on the login page.

User: Can I change my email address?
Support: Yes! Go to Account Settings → Email, and you can update it there.

User: What is the refund policy?
Support: You can request a refund within 30 days of purchase, as long as your account is in good standing.

User: Is there a mobile app?
Support: Yes, we have apps for both iOS and Android. Search for “OurApp” in your app store.

User: I’m seeing error code 403.
Support: Error 403 usually means permission denied. Please check if you're using the correct credentials.



=== Company Policy & FAQ ===
Work From Home Policy:
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days a week. Exceptions require manager approval.

Parental Leave:
We offer 16 weeks of paid parental leave for primary caregivers, and 6 weeks for secondary caregivers.

Health Insurance:
Benefits begin on the 1st day of the month following your start date. We offer medical, dental, and vision plans.

Vacation Days:
Full-time employees receive 15 vacation days per year, plus 10 paid holidays.

IT Support:
If you're facing technical issues, email helpdesk@company.com or call extension 411.

Office Hours:
Our standard office hours are 9 AM – 5 PM, Monday to Friday. Flex schedules are available upon approval.
